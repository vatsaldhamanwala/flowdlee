# Flowdlee Problem Statement

Many IT professionals, developers, and remote employees get deeply involved in work and forget to take healthy breaks during long working hours. This can lead to stress, fatigue, eye strain, back discomfort, and reduced focus.

Flowdlee helps users plan their day, run focused work sessions, receive smart break reminders, and reflect on their productivity and wellness patterns.

## Target Users

Initial target users:

- Software developers
- IT professionals
- Remote employees
- Freelancers
- Hybrid workers

Future expansion:

- Healthcare professionals
- Finance professionals
- Education professionals
- Other knowledge workers

## Core Product Rhythm

Plan → Focus → Pause → Reflect

## Product Positioning

Flowdlee is a calm, AI-ready, SaaS-style workday planner and smart break reminder platform.
